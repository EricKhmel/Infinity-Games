|Wii Sports

Wii Sports is a classic. It is a collection of five sports simulations, designed to demonstrate the motion-sensing capabilities of the Wii Remote. The five sports included are tennis, baseball, bowling, golf and boxing. Players use the Wii Remote to mimic actions performed in real-life sports, such as swinging a tennis racket, golf club, or rolling a bowling ball. The rules for each game are simplified to make them more accessible to new players. The game also features training and fitness modes that monitor players' progress in the sports. With over 82 million copies sold, Wii Sports is the the best selling single-platform game of all time and the fourth best-selling game of all time.
