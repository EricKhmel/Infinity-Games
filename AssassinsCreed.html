|Assassin's Creed

Assassin's Creed is an action-adventure video game that takes place primarily during the Third Crusade in the Holy Land in 1191. The player character is a modern-day man named Desmond Miles who, through a machine called the "Animus", relives the genetic memories of his ancestor, Altaïr Ibn-La'Ahad. Gameplay focuses on using Altaïr's combat, stealth, and parkour abilities to defeat enemies and explore the environment.
